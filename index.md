## About Me
I am a CS Ph.D. candidate at [Beijing Jiaotong University](https://bjtu.edu.cn/) (BJTU), advised by Prof. [Liping Jing](http://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/8249/) and Prof. [Jian Yu](http://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/6463/). My research focuses on machine learning and NLP, especially **weakly supervised / non-perfect data learning** for **multi-label text classification** and **multi-turn conversational LLMs**. I also build and evaluate real-world dialogue/voice-agent systems (ASR+LLM+TTS) and related benchmarks.

*Last updated: 2026-02*

***

## Research Interests
- Weak supervision, noisy/missing labels, and robustness for multi-label text classification
- Long-tailed / extreme multi-label learning and imbalanced classification
- Multi-turn conversational LLMs: evaluation, user simulation, and RAG-based dialogue
- Voice agents and intelligent outbound calling (ASR+LLM+TTS) with real-world benchmarks
- Tag/hashtag recommendation, keyphrase generation, and cross-modal learning
- Transportation & time-series forecasting applications
