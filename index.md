## About Me
I am a CS Ph.D. candidate at [Beijing Jiaotong University](https://bjtu.edu.cn/) (BJTU), Beijing, China, advised by Prof. [Liping Jing](http://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/8249/) and Prof. [Jian Yu](http://faculty.bjtu.edu.cn/6463/).

My research interests lie in machine learning, natural language processing and large language models (LLMs), with a focus on **weakly supervised learning**, **multi-label learning**, and **multi-turn conversational LLMs**.  

On the industrial side, I have developed **LLM-based outbound sales systems** and **evaluation benchmarks** for real-world telephone marketing scenarios (ASR+LLM+TTS), and applied machine learning to **container image recognition**, **OCR**, and **railway time-series forecasting**.

I enjoy academic discussions and collaborations. In our lab, I lead a small group on **multi-label learning and its applications**, mentoring several master students on label–text alignment, tag recommendation and cross-modal tag recommendation.
